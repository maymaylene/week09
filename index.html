Washington State

When people talk about Washington they automatically assume one is talking about our Nations capital, Washington D.C. However that is not always the case! Washington is a state in the United States that sits at the very top of the Pacific Northwest. It is know for its rainy and gloomy wheather. What many dont know is that Washington state has a lot of natural beauty. If you enjoy going on hikes and hanging out at the lake, then Washington is the perfect state for you!

This website was created in hopes of giving you a beif glimpse into the beauty of Washington state.
